Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play the dice game?

Click on the "Play Game" tab, enter your name, and roll the dice. Win if the total is 7 or 11!

Is this game free?

Yes, the game is completely free to play!

Where are my scores stored?

Your scores are securely stored in AWS DynamoDB, ensuring privacy and accessibility.

Who created this game?

The game was created as a project to demonstrate AWS cloud technology by Karis Iwobho.
