How to Play

Welcome to the Dice Game! Here are the rules:

  • Enter your name and start the game.
  • Click "Roll Dice" to roll two virtual dice.
  • If the total of the dice is 7 or 11, you win!
  • If the total is anything else, you lose. Try again for another chance to win!
